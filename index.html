Welcome to the Chemical Reaction Simulator!

Insert a chemical reaction and the website will output n(t), C(t) (if appropriate) and U(t) plots by simulating the reaction.

If a substance doesn't have a coefficient enter "1" as its coefficient.
For now we assume that all reactions are simple.
